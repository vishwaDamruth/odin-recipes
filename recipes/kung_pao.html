Description

Kung Pao Chicken is a popular Chinese stir-fry dish made with chicken, peanuts, and vegetables,
all tossed in a savory, spicy sauce. It's known for its combination of salty, sweet, and spicy flavors.

  • Checken Breast
  • Peanuts

Steps

  2. Cook chicken
  3. Stir fry veggies
